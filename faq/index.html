FAQ

Asked and answered

When will NoterBox be available?

NoterBox is still under development. Right now, I am doing less on the coding side and more on getting pre-seed investment.

If you would like to stay in the loop, you can subscribe using the form at the bottom of this page.

Will NoterBox be another journaling app?

NoterBox aims to be what we call a life-journaling app.

Compared to what’s out there currently, it differs in terms of vision, execution, usability and purpose.

You can have a look at the features.

Why not use another journaling app?

While I believe that Evernote is currently one of the best apps out there for capturing and managing data (at least at the time of writing) - it does not meet my requirements in terms of usability, vision and overall purpose. That is why I am building NoterBox - a Personal Information Management (PIM) platform.
