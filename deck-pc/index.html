What Is NoterBox?

NoterBox is a Personal Cloud Appliance

  • Sync photos and videos from your phone. Capture notes, files, web-clips, journal entries, reminders. Whatever feels important.
  • A secure device to serve as long-term unified storage.
  • NoterBox becomes your vault. Your life-stream. Tells your story.

The Problem:

  • Privacy & Full Control over content: if you are looking for a personal long-term storage solution for family photos, videos or documents, there is not much choice. Most solutions are SaaS cloud based with recurring payments
  • Availability - average person with a smartphone takes 2 to 3 photos per day, adding up to **1.2 trillion** photos globally - where do all these get stored ?
  • Digital Clutter takes over in time (duplication, irrelevant info, etc) - photos & documents everywhere
  • In time, data becomes difficult to find & manage. "Moments" are lost.

Meet NoterBox

  • NoterBox provides full privacy and control, unifying all your data in one single place where you can easily manage and find it. A single centralized storage location to "rule them all"
  • Put a stop to reccuring payments for managing your data. You can always use the "cloud" as backup
  • Sync all your photos and videos with NoterBox straight from your mobile device. Share them with family & friends!
  • Have all your information available when needed - even years later!

What Is The "Secret Sauce"?

With emerging technologies - a few attempts of building similar apps are noticeable. They either offer partial features or are lacking some. Regardless of the stack used, we feel none have the "right balance" as a whole.
What is the "Secret Sauce" in our vision?
  • Users should have full control over their data. One of the most requested features.
  • Make it easy for users to sync, share, organize and view their data (eg: connect the appliance to TV)
  • Cross-platform: make it easy for users to capture data from multiple sources with emphasize on desktop integration and web clipping functionality

Key Differentiators

  • Full privacy & control Delivered as a personal appliance, content is stored on customer premises, with full control over data with the option to share
  • No recurring payments A one time purchase (with options to upgrade)
  • AI / ML Technologies like AI and Machine Learning can be employed for automatic tagging/recognition based on metadata or geolocation - and also to provide advanced search capabilities

Market Validation

- We may never beat Google's pricing on data storage, our "ace in the sleeve" is Privacy. A proper market valiation needs to be conducted, but this is what we have so far:

  • A simple search on Google will show the lack of solutions. It will also show demand - and that that people are trying to create their own “systems” in order to capture and store data

- To be determined:

  • - Would people really pay for such a device to have it in their homes?
  • - How many people? How much are they willing to pay?

Market Size

  • We believe that the best market fit is the consumer market (B2C), having as main segment customers aged 35 - 50 yo
  • Sales from optional offerings: optional online backup, additional features, storage, or connected devices (picture frames, e-ink boards, scanners, etc)
  • DeGoogle movement: people are looking to break free from the Google ecosystem. Google will soon start disabling accounts unused for more than 2 years and discontinue free unlimited photo storage.
  • These will most likely increase demand for a similar service, causing people to look around for alternatives, ideally where they have privacy and full control over content
